# Humanizer Skill

Remove signs of AI-generated writing from text to make it sound more natural and human-written.

## When to Use
- Editing written content that sounds too "AI-like"
- Removing promotional language
- Fixing overused patterns (em dashes, rule of three, etc.)

## AI Writing Patterns to Fix
- Inflated symbolism
- Promotional language ("powerful", "seamless", "robust")
- Superficial -ing analyses
- Vague attributions
- Em dash overuse
- Rule of three abuse
- AI vocabulary words
- Negative parallelisms
- Excessive conjunctive phrases

## Process
1. Identify AI patterns in text
2. Replace with natural alternatives
3. Preserve original meaning
4. Maintain appropriate tone

## Output
- Original text with AI patterns removed
- List of changes made
- Explanation of improvements
